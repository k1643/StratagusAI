
The "maps" directory contains some stratagus maps that can be used for evaluating AI agents.
The intention is to have some standard maps that people can use to compare the
performance of different agents.

ballista_vs_tower: A force of 10 footmen and a ballista have to destroy a force of
10 enemy footman and a cannon tower.  A solution likely requires guarding the 
ballista while it attacks the tower.
